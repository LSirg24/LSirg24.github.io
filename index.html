About Us

At Paws and Whiskers Shelter, we believe every animal deserves a second chance. For over two decades, we have worked tirelessly to rescue and rehabilitate animals, providing them with the care and love they need. We are proud to have connected countless pets with their forever homes. Join us in making a difference today!

Support Our Shelter

Your support helps us provide food, medical care, and shelter to animals in need. There are many ways to get involved: volunteer, foster a pet, or donate. Together, we can give these animals a brighter future.
